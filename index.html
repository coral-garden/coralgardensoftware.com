What we build

We work with small businesses that have outgrown workarounds, awkward processes, or software that almost fits. Sometimes that means a new product. Sometimes it means a better internal system. Sometimes it means using AI in a way that is genuinely helpful rather than merely fashionable.

SaaS products

We build SaaS products from the ground up, and we also help tidy up the ones that began life as a quick prototype and are now expected to behave like proper software.

Internal tools

We make internal tools for businesses that are tired of bending themselves around generic software, spreadsheets, and manual routines that should have been retired some time ago.

Practical AI integration

We use AI where it earns its keep: reducing repetitive work, making information easier to find, and adding useful capability without turning the whole business into a science project.

Our approach

We try to be sensible about things. That means asking awkwardly practical questions, paying attention to the details, and building software that works in real life rather than only in a sales deck.

What you can expect

  • Plain speaking and sensible recommendations
  • Modern tools used for a reason, not for show
  • Software that is pleasant to use and straightforward to maintain
  • Careful implementation with proper attention to the details

What we avoid

  • Buzzwords doing the work of actual thinking
  • AI theatre and promises no sensible person should trust
  • Needless complexity dressed up as sophistication
  • Shortcuts that save a week now and cost six months later

We make serious software for small businesses, and we try not to make a song and dance about it.
